About Us

Welcome to Italio's your local Italian Cuisine Restaurant. Founded in 1949 in Brooklyn, New York. We are a family run restaraunt and we pride ourselves on our connection to our sicilian roots. We hope that these cultural influences shine through our food, and bring joy and warmth to those who consume.
